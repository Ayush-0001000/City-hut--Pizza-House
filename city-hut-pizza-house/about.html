    Vision

    Born as Cafe Pizza House on September 24, 2020, and reborn with fresh spirit as City Hut Pizza House on March 23, 2023, we carry forward our journey with the promise—We are serving the freeness—a story of flavor, resilience, and community that keeps growing with every slice.

    Our mission Our mission is to craft delicious pizzas with fresh ingredients and warm hospitality, creating a place where people connect and experience the spirit of freeness.

    Material Quality

    At City Hut Pizza House, we believe that true freeness begins with uncompromising quality—every dough is hand‑prepared, every topping carefully selected, and every ingredient sourced with freshness and authenticity in mind, so that each slice not only satisfies hunger but also delivers trust, flavor, and the joy of sharing something made with care.

    Rigorous standards ensure every pizza meets the highest taste and safety requirements.

    Freshness

    At City Hut Pizza House, freshness is our heartbeat—every morning begins with hand‑prepared dough, crisp vegetables, and carefully chosen ingredients, so that the aroma, taste, and spirit of freeness greet every guest the moment they step inside.

    We prioritize speed and quality to deliver a truly fresh meal every time.
